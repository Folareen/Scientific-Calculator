1.Always add brackets to the end of every functions before inputting values.
i.e sin(90) NOT sin90

2.Add brackets to functions that doesn't need values too.
i.e pi() NOT p

3.Use multiplication(*) in place of brackets when you want to multiply functions by functions or numbers.
i.e 6 * sin(90) NOT 6(sin(90))

3.Single click CE to clear input.

4.Double click CE to clear input, result and error.

5.Do not add unnecessary zeros to numbers.
i.e 567 NOT 0567.
